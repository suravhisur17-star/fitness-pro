    💡 Tips to Stay Safe Online
    🔒
    Always Check HTTPS
    Never enter passwords or payment info on sites without the padlock icon. Look for https:// at the start.
    🕵️
    Hover Before Clicking
    Hover your mouse over links to preview the real destination URL in your browser's status bar before clicking.
    📧
    Beware Urgent Emails
    Phishers create false urgency — "Your account will be closed!" Legitimate companies never pressure you this way.
    🧩
    Check the Domain
    Phishing sites use lookalike domains like paypa1.com or g00gle.com. Read carefully!
    🔑
    Use a Password Manager
    Password managers won't autofill credentials on fake sites — a natural layer of phishing protection built in.
    🛡️
    Enable 2FA Everywhere
    Two-factor authentication prevents attackers from accessing your account even if they steal your password.
